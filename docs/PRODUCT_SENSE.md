# Product Sense

Owner: @product
Last Reviewed: 2026-02-20
Status: active

## Purpose

Ensure implementation choices remain anchored to user outcomes.

## Product Questions To Answer

- Who is the user?
- What pain are we removing?
- What does success look like?
- What is explicitly out of scope?
- What are the failure modes?

## Agent Workflow

- Translate user feedback into acceptance criteria.
- Prioritize by user impact and reversibility.
- Keep specs updated as behavior changes.

## Required Artifacts

- Product specs in `docs/product-specs/`.
- Quality updates in `docs/QUALITY_SCORE.md`.
- Follow-up plans for unresolved product risks.
