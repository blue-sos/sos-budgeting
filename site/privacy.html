Who We Are

SOS Budgeting is an internal finance tool operated by School of Song to sync accounting data from QuickBooks into Google Sheets and Firebase Firestore.

Data We Access

  • QuickBooks accounting records authorized through OAuth.
  • OAuth metadata (realm ID, token timestamps, and connection status).
  • Operational sync metadata and raw report payloads needed for audit and replay.

How We Use Data

  • Generate budgeting and reporting sheets.
  • Maintain run logs for reliability and troubleshooting.
  • Prevent duplicate sync writes via idempotent run keys.

Data Sharing

We do not sell data. Data is shared only with infrastructure providers required to operate the internal workflow (Google Cloud and Intuit APIs).

Data Retention

Sync outputs and raw payloads are retained for operational finance history and audit purposes. Retention windows may be adjusted by School of Song based on policy and legal requirements.

Security

  • Secrets are stored outside source control.
  • Access to credentials and stores is role-restricted.
  • API calls are authenticated via OAuth and service-account controls.

Your Choices

You may revoke app access from QuickBooks at any time. If revoked, new sync jobs will stop until re-authorized.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact blue@schoolofsong.org.
