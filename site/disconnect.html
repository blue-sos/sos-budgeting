Disconnect Instructions

SOS Budgeting QuickBooks Integration

Home Privacy Terms Launch

To disconnect this integration, revoke app access from the connected QuickBooks company and remove local stored refresh tokens.

Disconnect Steps

  1. In QuickBooks Online, open Apps, then locate this integration and disconnect it.
  2. Delete local OAuth tokens from your machine or secret manager.
  3. Confirm future sync runs fail until explicit re-authorization.
